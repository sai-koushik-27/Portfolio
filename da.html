Maven Fuzzy Factory

Tools:

SQL,Power BI


Maven Fuzzy Factory is a dynamic e-commerce startup specializing in creating and selling high-quality, customizable stuffed animals. Our project focuses on analyzing user behavior, traffic sources, and product performance using SQL to derive actionable insights for improving our business strategy and operations.

By understanding how users interact with our website, identifying the most effective traffic channels, and assessing product performance, we aim to enhance customer satisfaction, increase revenue, and streamline operational efficiency. Our key features include customizable dashboards, comprehensive reporting, and predictive analytics, all geared towards supporting data-driven decision-making and fostering business growth.

Electoral Bond Data Analysis

Tools:

SQL


The Electoral Bond Project is a comprehensive initiative aimed at analyzing data on anonymous political donations in India. By leveraging SQL and other advanced data analysis tools, this project seeks to uncover funding patterns and political contributions, providing valuable insights into the financial landscape of Indian politics.

The project focuses on collecting, processing, and analyzing data related to electoral bonds, which are financial instruments used for making anonymous donations to political parties. The goal is to identify trends, patterns, and anomalies in political funding, ultimately promoting transparency and accountability in the electoral process. Key features include interactive dashboards, detailed reporting, and predictive analytics, all geared towards supporting data-driven decision-making and fostering transparency and accountability in political funding.
